Why the CVC Exchange is harder to support than a typical SaaS platform.

The CVC Exchange isn't one system ‚Äî it's an API-mediated network connecting 100+ colleges, each with their own SIS configuration, FA rules, Canvas environment, and compliance calendar. A single enrollment failure can cascade: no SIS record means no Canvas access, which means no FA disbursement, which means a student loses their aid.

I understand this chain because I've worked at multiple points in it. At SMCCD I saw what happens downstream when upstream data is wrong. At Rice I saw what Canvas access failures look like to a student who can't get into their course. At SCC Schools I learned how platform bugs propagate differently across institutions with different technical capacity.

Supporting the CVC Exchange means understanding the dependencies, not just the tickets. That's the work I'm built for.
