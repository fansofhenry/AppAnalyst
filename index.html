Data Integrity (SQL)

Identifying "Orphaned Enrollments" where the student exists in the Exchange but lacks a matching OpenCCCID in the local SIS.

SELECT e.enrollment_id, s.last_name
FROM cvc_exchange.enrollments e
LEFT JOIN sis.student_master s 
  ON e.student_id = s.external_id
WHERE s.open_ccc_id IS NULL;
